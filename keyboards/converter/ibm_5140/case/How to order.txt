The case is made out of 5 layers:
- Base layer
- Middle layer 1
- Middle layer 2
- Top layer
- Pen holder

How to order the different parts of the case:
1- Base layer: 3mm thickness
2- Middle layer 1: 6mm thickness
3- Middle layer 2: 3mm thickness
4- Top layer: 8mm thickness

NB: The top layer contains two pieces: the top layer and the pen holder.

Screw and spaces:
1- 9x M3 Aluminum spacers 20mm length (https://a.aliexpress.com/_mOULKUI)
2- 4x M3 Aluminum spacers 25mm length (https://a.aliexpress.com/_mOULKUI)
3- 26x M3 Hex screws 10mm length (https://a.aliexpress.com/_mtbwIFG)
